Welcome to sport club


The Department of Sport Science and Physical Education of the University of Kelaniya provides a multi-faceted environment that delivers high quality education for those aspiring to join the sport and recreation industry. Our staff members provide student centered education through well-arranged student centered learning materials. Our academic staff members also engage in a range of basic, exploratory and explanatory research activities, often with both national and international collaboration and publication in high impact sport and recreational journals. All of these contribute towards creating an extremely conducive student centered learning environment for undergraduates who looking forward to the sport and recreation industry.
